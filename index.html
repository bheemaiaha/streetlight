STREETLIGHT

Bicycle + Smartphone + Cardboard Projector

Streetlight is smartphone app which turns your night bicycle ride into a shining experience.

Begin with Three Steps

  1. Get Mini Cardboard Smartphone Projector and mount it on bicycle handlebars.
  2. Launch Streetlight app on your smartphone, choose one of the mods and insert your smartphone into the Cardboard Projector.
  3. Enjoy your ride.

Warning: You are strongly advised to use regular bicycle front+rear light along with the Streetlight app to make sure you are properly visible in night traffic.

Streetlight Mods

Icons near each mod tell you which smartphone features are required to use the mod (e.g., internet, gps).
  • Speed – Your actual speed, visualized both as white bar and as number. Scroll down the main menu to switch from metric to imperial units.
  • Streetname – Name of the street you are currently driving through. Use to learn streetnames of your city.
  • Grid – Lumigrids-like effect. Use to avoid holes in roads.
  • Map – Map of your neighborhood.
  • Knight Rider Mode – Tribute to K.I.T.T. car light.
  • Matrix Mode – Tribute to the Matrix movie. The faster you move, the faster the green glyphs move too.

Add Your Own Mod

Streetlight source code is hosted on github. The application is written in Ionic framework and anyone familiar with JavaScript should be able to add his own mod.
